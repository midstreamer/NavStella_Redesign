LEADERSHIP

Divya Visentini

Managing Partner

For nearly 20 years, Divya has been crafting marketing and experience strategies driven by technology for Fortune 500 and other Enterprise and mid-market clients. She has orchestrated national and global programs and campaigns for brands like Shell, Chevron, NRG, Direct Energy, HP, Microsoft, Sony, AIG, Bank of America, GE Capital, Wells Fargo, SunGard, Travelex, Citi Group, Continental Airlines, Bausch + Lomb, and Red Bull. A change maker, she has always sought to drive innovation and business growth for her clients.

Before starting NavStella, Divya Visentini was Managing Director at Accenture and served as North America Transformation and Innovation lead for the Oil and Gas vertical.

She came to Accenture from Hearst. At Hearst, she started the digital agency GTM and delivery models that resulted in a tri-coastal agency market presence for Hearst including Amuse Digital in Houston.

A speaker, a change maker, guide, and mentor – Divya has always sought to create a balanced, happy, and productive work environment, and influences the people around her with positivity and passion.

Divya is a member of the board at SEARCH Homeless, a Houston-based organization working on ending homelessness in Houston. She is also a member of the advisory board for SMU’s digital accelerator.

Divya holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Biochemistry (Bangalore University, India), an MBA in Corporate Finance & Strategy (Hull University, UK), and another MBA in Marketing (University of Houston). Go Coogs!

She is a mother to a 14-year-old boy and a Shih Tzu named Peanut. A cricket and soccer enthusiast, she is a die-hard supporter of Arsenal Football Club. A self-proclaimed “aspiring polymath”, she enjoys many things but for now she is focused on reading, art, travel, and learning new languages. She is a keen advocate of Diversity & Inclusion initiatives and is active in helping drive education among refugees settling in our lovely city.

Chad Alessi

Managing Partner

Chad works with his client partners to drive value creation and cost savings, through leadership in reliability, data, and technology. Embracing NavStella’s core values of integrity, innovation, and excellence, Chad has consistently developed teams and solutions for complex business problems. This has allowed the companies that he has worked with to create environments of collaboration, and a proactive approach to their businesses, resulting in cost savings derived from operational and process efficiencies.

Chad holds an MBA in Data Analytics and a Bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Alabama. Roll Tide! He has certifications in Leadership from Harvard Business School, Predictive Analytics from Northwestern University, Full Stack Development from University of Texas at Austin, and is a Certified Reliability Engineer, Certified Process Safety Professional, and IoT Product Manager.

Chad is an active member of the Society of Manufacturing Engineers, American Institute of Chemical Engineers, and The American Society of Quality.

He is a former Director of Operations with Williams Companies. A Principal Engineer and Project Manager with Kinder Morgan, Project Engineer with Aker Kvaener, and Production Engineer with NuCor Steel.

Chad served in the United States Marine Corps and is a proud father of two accomplished and beautiful daughters, and 2 year old Labradoodle called Teddy.

Although a Crimson Tide fan, Chad is an all-round avid college football enthusiast. He also loves reading, photography, drawing, and traveling. A keen advocate of women’s and children’s rights, Chad volunteers and participates in programs and projects supporting women and children.
